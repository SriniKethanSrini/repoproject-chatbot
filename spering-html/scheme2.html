Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)

Description

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) was launched in June 2015 with the aim of providing affordable housing to all urban and rural households by 2022.

Key Features:

PMAY offers financial assistance to eligible beneficiaries for the construction, purchase, or enhancement of houses.

The scheme is divided into two components: Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) and Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Gramin), targeting urban and rural areas respectively.

Subsidies are provided on home loans for economically weaker sections (EWS), low-income groups (LIG), and middle-income groups (MIG) to make housing affordable.

PMAY aims to promote sustainable and affordable housing with amenities like water supply, sanitation, electricity, and access to livelihood opportunities.

Application Process: Interested individuals can apply for PMAY through designated banks, housing finance companies, or online portals. Applicants need to fulfill eligibility criteria based on income, property ownership, and other parameters.

Promotion of Electronic Payments: PMJDY encourages the use of digital payment methods such as debit cards, mobile banking, and electronic fund transfers, fostering a cashless economy and reducing reliance on cash transactions.
